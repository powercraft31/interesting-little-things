## One-paragraph summary

v6.10.1 is a storage-governance hardening release layered on top of v6.10 runtime governance. It keeps v6.10's runtime spine intact, adds deterministic Docker log caps, classifies append-heavy operational tables by meaning, and routes each governed surface into one honest lifecycle: hard-cap, hot-window retention, archive, or intentional indefinite preservation. The design explicitly refuses to treat business history such as `revenue_daily` as disposable cleanup residue.
